About

Well, hello there! If you couldn't tell from the big, bright heading, my name is Naomi Lounsbury. I am a budding developer who took the roundabout road to the developer path.

Before getting into my super exciting education background, you should probably know a little about me. My hobbies include hiking, traveling and reading the trashiest romance, science fiction and fantasy around. If its absurd with gapping plot holes, I'm into it. I love politics with a passion and will fact-check everything, we have all of human knowledge at our fingertips so why not fact-check Great Aunt Helen's belief that vaccines cause infertility. I'm also an extrovert and an Aquarius. No, I don't actually believe in astrology but I also don't not believe in it. I've written horoscopes for two years and its all about hindsight bias and getting enough people to corroborate vagaries.

In college, I double majored in Anthropology and Chinese thinking that the job opportunities would just be flowing in.

Haha, no such luck.

After my illustrious college career, I went off to China for eight years. Yes, you read that right, eight years. While in China, I earned my Masters in Teaching Chinese as a Second Language, taught kindergarten and wrote for a magazine. That was a grand time but while it was an easy life, it was not a career.

Ultimately, I decided to leave China to pursue a life outside the bubble of China. Sadly my job skills were lacking but upon reaching the golden shores of California, I enrolled in the UC Berkeley Extension program for coding and I'm now on my way to becoming a fullstack developer.
